User Data Deletion Instructions

If you signed in to Radha Naam Jap using your Facebook account and wish to delete your data, please follow the steps below.

  1. Open the Radha Naam Jap app in your browser.
  2. Go to the Settings section (bottom navigation).
  3. Tap Sign Out to disconnect your account.
  4. To permanently delete all your data, send an email to:
    drakthephenomenal@gmail.com
    with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request — Radha Naam Jap"

We will delete all your data from our servers within 30 days of receiving your request.

What data we store: We only store your jap count history, mala targets, and preferences in Firebase (Google Cloud). We do not store any payment information or sensitive personal data.
